The following tests are needed for AES-GCM

Keys
----
1. Legacy AES GCM key
2. New AES GCM key only
3. Combined legacy and new AES GCM key, legacy active
4. Combined legacy and new AES GCM key, new is active

TESTS
-----
1. All keys decrypt what they encrypted
2. (3) decrypts anything encrypted with (1) and (2)
3. (4) decrypts anything encrypted with (1) and (2)
4. (1) does not decrypt what was encrypted with (4)
5. (2) does not decrypt what was encrypted with (3)
